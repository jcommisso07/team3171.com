About

Aspiring High School students take part in the FIRST Robotics program, yielding them unique educational opportunities and technical experiences. Our team, 3171, was founded in 2009 by just a few high school students. Since then, our student-run team has grown exponentially and distinguished itself across the nation and the world. Our coach, Tony Kryl, a 2016 Woodie Flowers award recipient, led our team to the 2013 and 2015 World Championships in St. Louis.
